Welcome to Burnsville

Nestled in the Blue Ridge Mountains, Burnsville offers outdoor adventure, arts, and small-town charm. Whether you’re here to hike Mount Mitchell, explore local crafts, or just relax, you’ll find plenty to enjoy.

Highlights

  • Mount Mitchell State Park – Highest peak east of the Mississippi.
  • Toe River Arts – Local galleries & Appalachian craft traditions.
  • Bare Dark Sky Observatory – Stargazing under some of the darkest skies in the Southeast.
