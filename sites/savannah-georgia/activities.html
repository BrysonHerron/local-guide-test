Home Attractions Activities Food & Drink Hotels

Savannah, Georgia, enchants visitors with its rich tapestry of history, stunning architecture, and vibrant Southern culture. Explore the city's iconic squares, indulge in delectable Lowcountry cuisine, and immerse yourself in the lively arts scene, all while wandering cobblestone streets lined with moss-draped oaks. Experience unique ghost tours, historic plantations, and the evocative rhythm of riverboats on the Savannah River for an unforgettable journey through this charming coastal gem.
