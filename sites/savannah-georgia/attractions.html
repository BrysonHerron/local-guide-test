Savannah, Georgia, captivates visitors with its rich tapestry of history and vibrant culture, where cobblestone streets are lined with majestic oak trees draped in Spanish moss. Explore the city's iconic squares, indulge in mouthwatering Southern cuisine, and immerse yourself in the arts scene that thrives within its historic architecture, making every moment a blend of charm and discovery. Unique ghost tours and riverboat cruises offer unforgettable ways to experience the city's enchanting spirit and storied past.
