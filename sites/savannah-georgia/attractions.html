Attractions in Savannah, Georgia

Home Attractions Activities Food & Drink Hotels

Savannah, Georgia, is a vibrant tapestry of history and culture, where cobblestone streets lead to lush, historic squares and grand antebellum architecture. Visitors can relish the unique flavors of Southern cuisine, from mouthwatering low-country boil to delectable pralines, while exploring the city's rich artistic scene, complete with charming galleries and lively street performances. Experience the haunting beauty of ghost tours or unwind in the serene parks, all of which weave together to create an unforgettable Southern escape.
