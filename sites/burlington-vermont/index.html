Top Attractions

Burlington, Vermont, offers a vibrant mix of outdoor adventures and rich cultural experiences, all set against the stunning backdrop of Lake Champlain. Explore the lively Church Street Marketplace, where local artisans and delicious farm-to-table eateries create a unique flavor, or immerse yourself in the arts at the Burlington City Arts Center, showcasing everything from contemporary exhibitions to community events. For a taste of Burlington’s natural beauty, venture out to nearby hiking trails or bike along the scenic waterfront, making every moment an unforgettable adventure.

Food & Drink

Burlington, Vermont, is a culinary gem where farm-to-table freshness meets craft brew mastery.
