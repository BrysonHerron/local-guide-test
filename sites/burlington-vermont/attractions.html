Attractions in Burlington, Vermont

Home Attractions Activities Food & Drink Hotels

Burlington, Vermont, offers a unique blend of outdoor adventure and vibrant cultural experiences, nestled between the stunning shores of Lake Champlain and the Green Mountains. Visitors can explore a thriving arts scene, indulge in farm-to-table dining with local craft beers, and stroll along the charming Church Street Marketplace, all while reveling in the city's innovative spirit and natural beauty. Whether kayaking on the lake or enjoying a live performance at a local venue, Burlington provides unmatched experiences that capture the essence of Vermont living.
