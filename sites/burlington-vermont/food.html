Food in Burlington, Vermont

Home Attractions Activities Food & Drink Hotels

Burlington, Vermont, is a culinary delight where farm-to-table freshness meets vibrant local flavors. Nestled between the shores of Lake Champlain and the Green Mountains, the city boasts a thriving food scene filled with artisanal products, craft breweries, and cozy cafés. Whether you're indulging in a handcrafted cheese board or sipping on a locally brewed craft beer, Burlington invites you to savor its diverse palate in a charming, community-focused atmosphere.
