Activities in Burlington, Vermont

Home Attractions Activities Food & Drink Hotels

Nestled between the serene shores of Lake Champlain and the vibrant Green Mountains, Burlington, Vermont, offers a diverse range of activities that cater to all interests. Visitors can explore the bustling local arts scene, savor farm-to-table cuisine at charming eateries, and enjoy outdoor adventures such as hiking and biking along picturesque trails. The city's unique blend of laid-back charm and vibrant cultural energy makes it the perfect destination for exploration and relaxation.
