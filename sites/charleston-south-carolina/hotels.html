Hotels in Charleston, South Carolina

Charleston, South Carolina offers a captivating array of accommodations that reflect its rich history and vibrant culture, from charming historic inns nestled in the heart of the old city to modern boutique hotels with stunning waterfront views. Guests can savor the local flavor at unique bed-and-breakfasts that serve traditional Lowcountry cuisine, or immerse themselves in Southern hospitality at grand antebellum estates. Each stay provides an opportunity to experience the city's renowned hospitality and deep-rooted charm.
