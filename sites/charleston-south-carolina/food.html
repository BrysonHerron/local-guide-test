Food in Charleston, South Carolina

Home Attractions Activities Food & Drink Hotels

Charleston, South Carolina, is a culinary treasure trove where vibrant flavors meet rich traditions, offering an unforgettable dining experience steeped in Southern hospitality. Savor the unique blend of Lowcountry cuisine, from shrimp and grits to she-crab soup, and explore the city's dynamic beverage scene featuring craft cocktails and local brews, all set against the backdrop of historic charm and coastal beauty. Each dish tells a story, inviting you to indulge in the cultural tapestry that makes Charleston a true foodie haven.
