Key West, Florida, is a vibrant island paradise known for its stunning sunsets, rich history, and laid-back atmosphere. Visitors can indulge in an array of activities, from snorkeling in crystal-clear waters and exploring the lively Duval Street to savoring fresh seafood at local eateries and immersing themselves in the town's eclectic art scene. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences, Key West offers something for everyone in a uniquely charming setting.
