Whether you’re snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, exploring charming streets filled with pastel-hued cottages, or enjoying a sunset celebration at Mallory Square, this tropical paradise promises unforgettable experiences around every corner. Dive into the myriad activities that make Key West a unique destination, where every moment is an adventure waiting to unfold!
