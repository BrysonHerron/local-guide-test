Food in Portland, Maine

Home Attractions Activities Food & Drink Hotels

Nestled along the picturesque shores of Casco Bay, Portland, Maine, boasts a vibrant culinary scene that reflects its New England roots and maritime charm. Here, fresh seafood takes center stage, from succulent lobster rolls to briny oysters, all complemented by an array of local craft breweries and artisanal distilleries. Dive into the flavors of this coastal gem, where every meal tells a story of the land and sea.
